A self-taught, self-starter, I've been in love with code ever since my mum first came home with a Commodore Vic20 (remember them!?) in the early 80s.
My brother and I spent many hours faithfully copying huge, long, BASIC programs from magazines and library books. But, as much as I loved getting my head round GOSUB and Variables, I became a teenager and my priorities changed.
